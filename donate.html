Details about donations

Your quality donations do the environment good keeping We are pleased to accept quality, clean items in good repair. Please drop off items at 445 Chalmers during our donation hours. Donations left after hours become damaged by weather and people.

Donations pick-up:

We provide a FREE pick up service for donated furniture and appliances. All donations must be in clean, salable and in working condition. No rips, tears or obvious stains, smoke free and bug free. Please make sure all items to be picked up are on the main floor and are easily accessible for our drivers. Our drivers DO NOT pick up items in basements or higher floors unless there is an elevator. All donated items will be viewed and assessed by our drivers who have the right to refuse anything they deem unsalable. To make arrangements, to pick up your furniture and appliances, please call the store at 204 668-0967 ext 1 to speak with one of our staff or volunteers. You may also fill up donation pickup form below to be contacted by our staff

Thank you :)

We appreciate your generosity and suggested donation. There are several items we will not accept due to their lack of salability. These factors include the cleanliness and condition of Items. We do not accept anything with obvious rips, tears or stains as we need to consider sanitary standards. Also we need to discern weight restrictions, availability of storage space, as well as current trends. All of which equal little or no demand of certain items in our current market place. Our staff and volunteers are mindful of environmental issues and work hard to avoid adding more unnecessary waste to our landfills. Furthermore, these standard practices help us to reduce our costs so that we may have a greater financial impact on the work of MCC. Our drivers are qualified to use their discretion when picking up items and adhere to these guidelines. We appreciate your cooperation. Thank you.
