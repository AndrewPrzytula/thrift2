We provide a FREE pick up service for donated furniture and appliances.

All donations must be in clean, salable and in working condition.
No rips, tears or obvious stains, smoke free and bug free.

Please make sure all items to be picked up are on the main floor
and are easily accessible for our drivers.
Our drivers DO NOT pick up items in basements or higher floors
unless there is an elevator.
All donated items will be viewed and assessed by our drivers
who have the right to refuse anything they deem unsalable.
