About Kildonan Mcc Thrift Store

Nestled in the heart of East Kildonan, is a dignified thrift shopping experience for all.
Kildonan MCC Thrift shop sits at the corner of Watt and Chalmers, easily accessible by transit with multiple entrances off Watt and Chalmers.

We opened our doors in 1996 and we continue to strive to make a difference in our community of East Kildonan. Our upscale atmosphere is unlike any other thrift shop, yet our pricing and environmental goals are in line with all MCC Thrift Shops. We invite you to experience what thrift can be! Drop by and see the difference.

About KMTS

With over 350 volunteers who give generously of their time to sort, clean and price the donated product, we work hard to get affordable product onto the floor for our customers. We donate to local community organizations who provide programs, outreach and support to those who are vulnerable and in need. We provide employment opportunities for those who may struggled with employability skills.

Donate

Your quality donations do the environment good keeping We are pleased to accept quality, clean items in good repair. Please drop off items at 445 Chalmers during our donation hours. Donations left after hours become damaged by weather and people.

Volunteer

Hundreds of volunteers help with the day to day operations at the Kildonan MCC Thrift Shop. We’re always looking for volunteers to receive donations, sort/price items, merchandise and stage the items. We love to have friendly outgoing volunteers to be the face of Kildonan MCC Thrift by working with our customers. If you have a few hours a week or several days a month, please call or email us for details. We always appreciate volunteers with specific knowledge and talents to enhance our shop’s donation procedures.
